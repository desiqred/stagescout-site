About StageScout

StageScout is dedicated to bridging the gap between talented local musicians and venues looking for performers. We believe every artist deserves a stage and every venue deserves great talent.

Our platform makes it easy to discover open mics, gig nights, jam sessions, and showcases in your area. Whether you're a solo acoustic artist, a full band, or somewhere in between, we help you find the perfect opportunity to share your music.

Our Mission: To create a thriving local music ecosystem by connecting performers with performance spaces, fostering community, and celebrating live music.
